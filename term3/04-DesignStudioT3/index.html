    Design Studio class term 3¶

    Proposed first intervention¶

    With the water people (Ramiro & Cagsun), we already did a first intervention. We took part in Slow Lab’s third Solar Brunch with our current water filtration prototype (which we collaborated on with them, based on a STUDIO10 prototype as well as our own prototype from last term). We also brought a second prototype of our game, inn which participants are encouraged to re-imagine their domestic space as a place for greywater-based cultivation (e.g. mushroom farming, herbs, fermentation, etc). This led to many interesting and promising conversatiosn with different people and projects (including Low Tech magazine).

    Our next proposed intervention is doing the same thing at the same spot (Conneccthort), but at another event (on slow architecture). For that, we will prepare a slightly more refined version of the game, as well as - hopefully - a name for our current project, so that we can keep people updated through some form of social media.
