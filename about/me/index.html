    About¶

    My focus is currently on distributed design for urban cultivation and its potential to help us playfully produce what we need and truly want. More specifically, I’m trying to make urban cultivation (of plants, fungi, bacteria, yeasts, worms, etc) - in both domestic and public/collective spaces - easier, more desirable and more useful for fulfilling our needs and authentic desires.
